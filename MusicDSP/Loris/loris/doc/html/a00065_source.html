25  * Notifier.h
26  *
27  * A pair of dedicated streams, notifier and debugger, are used for
28  * notification throughout the Loris class library. These streams are used
29  * like cout or cerr, but they buffer their contents until a newline is
30  * receieved. Then they post their entire contents to a notification
31  * handler. The default handler just prints to stderr, but other handlers
32  * may be dynamically specified using setNotifierHandler() and
33  * setDebuggerHandler().
34  *
35  * debugger is enabled only when compiled with the preprocessor macro
36  * Debug_Loris defined. It cannot be enabled using setDebuggerHandler() if
37  * Debug_Loris is undefined.When Debug_Loris is not defined, characters
38  * streamed onto debugger are never posted nor are they otherwise
39  * accessible.
40  *
41  * Notifier.h may be included in c files. The stream declarations are
42  * omitted, but the notification handler routines are accessible.
43  *
44  *
45  * Kelly Fitz, 28 Feb 2000
46  * loris@cerlsoundgroup.org
47  *
48  * http://www.cerlsoundgroup.org/Loris/
49  *
50  */
